Hi friends kaise ho sab , I am upset with my life.... i dont have any confession to share but I have a problem, I m 22 a very friendly boy who loves to make new friends in life. Mai randomly kisi se bhi road pe baat kr leta hu and dosti bhi kr leta hu without thinking about their background or present life status work ,education etc but my parents are very much worried about me mai unfortunately ghar pe reh k hi padhai kar raha hu, isliye mere parents hamesha mujhpe watch rakhte h dont know why? Parents kehte h kitum kaise kaise ladko se dosti krte ho and unse mat milna ya baat mat karna aaj k baad they all will harm u, isliye mere parents mujhe na to ghar se nikalne dete h aur na hi mere kisi dost ko ghar me aane dete h, mai din bhar ek room ke baitha rehta hu mummy papa k paas mujhse baat karne ki time or interest dono hi nahi , mai awara ladka nahi hu and mai hamesha dhyan rakhta hu mai kisi galat sangat me na rahu kabhi, phir bhi mere parents ne mujhe daat k and kasam deke ek room me baitha k rakhte h , mai apne parents ko hurt nhi karna chahta isliye chup chap unka kehna maanta hu , but andar hi andar mai khatam ho raha hu, kisi se apni problem share nhi kr pata, life me friends ka sukh aaj tk nhi mila and shayad kabhi milega bhi nhi, please friends mujhe kuch suggest karo aap sab and God se wish karo ki mere parents ko meri problem and meri feelings samajh aaye..... I feel so lonely and helpless in my life without friends I feel hollow having nobody to share my joy or sorrow .....God bless all ......
