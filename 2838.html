I am aware that my views will transpire altercations, and dissents. Nonetheless, that does not thwart me from articulating my opinion. The whole definition of cheating in the perception of you people is having an intercourse with an individual beyond a relationship. I take strong exception from this myopic meaning and would love to expound it in another fashion. Indulging in an act of voluntary copulation with whomsoever it might be does not tantamount cheating in my considered view. A crook is a man who adulterates the quality of love and affection by deralicting and shunning his lover for someone else. It also includes those who fail to spare time for their partners and become nonchalant and lose passion in their love and choose to quarrel and drag the farce on.

hindi translation : mai janta hoon ki mere vichaar asantosh or jhagron ko badhawa denge . fir bhi yeh mujhe mere vichar saaf bolne se nahi roka sakta. cheating ki paribhasha tum logo ke vichaar me ek viyakti ka rishte se bahar sex kerna hai .

main is choti dharna ke ult hoon aur ise kisi aur roop me vyakt kerna chaahunga .

kise ke satth khud ki ichha se sambhogh/sex kerna meri nazar me cheating nahi hai .ek badmaash aadmi meri nazar me wo hai jo apne lover ko tiyaag ke pyar ko milawati bna deta hai . ye un logo ko bhi chapet me leta hai jo log apne lover ke liye vakt nahi naikalte , beperwah kism ke hote hain , ladai kerte hain or ise badhawa dete hain .
