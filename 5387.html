We've failed to remain the human beings we once were-- moving together from one place to another, settling down, interdependent on each other, and loving each other-- the ancient ones. Binaries are everywhere now: from man/woman/third gender/other to general/SC/ST/OBC, and ending in normal/abnormal. Marginalization is everywhere. Hatred is prevalent. Heterosexuals hate non-heterosexuals and homosexuals hate on non-homosexuals. Homophobia and heterophobia walk hand-in-hand. For once, reflect on your life; you'll find love is, but at the meanest corner of your heart. Let it come out and create a greener world where cherry blossoms touch your skin in Spring and smiles talk of life. Please.

GCI- Too hard it is. And it's very difficult to live with a neutral mindset. These things are everywhere.
