4101(Poem shared by a guy)

Give me a book to read, A pencil to sketch, A paper to write, So many things i admire, So many tears to wipe.

The falling drops of water, Wash away all my sins, I have a clear understanding Of beauty and all the other things.

I know myself better, Trying hard to communicate, I always knew i was different, Yet i feel the same.

The laws of nature are free, Shackels belong to the society, I am willing to break them And come out.

Knowing myself makes me happy, The news will break you, The tears will follow, For some things i know I am to blame, I blame myself for all the sorrow.

In the end you wil come around, I know you have to, There is nothing wrong with who I am, i am just being honest and true, I have to tell this to you I know I have to, For you have the right to know, Who did you give birth to.

The love will over come all this, I have hope in me, Just try and understand, I am just fighting to be me, I am just trying to be me..
