I want to get married and have a beautiful baby. I want to perform all the Hindu traditions and customs, but the only problem is that I have a man's body and I don't have the money to undergo sex reassignment operation.

GCI- I am so sorry for you. I cannot even understand how difficult it would be for you and other trans people who cannot undergo sex reassignment surgery.
