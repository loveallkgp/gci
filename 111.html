"(I LOVED MY ROOMMATE)

Jab main first year engg college mei tha to mere 2 roommates the. Tabi hamare room mei ek aur ladka aya aur hamare saath hi rehne laga. Mera sabse pehla roommate bada hi cute tha. Hum dono ne apne bed jod liye aur hum 3 do beds pe sone lage. Ek banda apne bed pe sota tha. Ek raat ko yu hua ki mera hath mere roommate ki chest pe lag geya aur usne apni taang meri ass pe rakh li. Main bahut excited hua. Fir roz raat ko aisa hi kuch hone laga. ek baar main seeda leta hua tha to usne apna ghutna mere dick pe laga liya aur apna hath mere tummy pe rakh liya. Meri saans badi garam ho gayi but aur kuch nai hua. Maine agle din mazak mein us se ye baat bol di. Uske bad se sab band ho geya aur usne apna group change kar liya. Hamare bich mein contact bilkul bi nai he or main use bohot miss karta hu. Shayad wo mera first love he."
