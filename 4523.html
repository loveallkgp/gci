GCI- Look at it from a different perspective. If he, God forbids, dies or have to move somewhere else or chooses not to talk with you again without giving you any reason, how would you feel? You would miss him like anything and you'd miss his love. You would then say that there would be nobody else who would love you like he did. You may not feel it now but if he goes away you'd definitely feel it. I know this because there have been confessions like yours in the past and people did feel what I just told you. Anyway., if you still don't feel anything then finish it off. It is not good at all to keep him on hope where there is no future of you both from your side.
