I am feeling really suffocated these days, all these years I have pretended to be a man. I have to speak like a man, dress up like a man, make friends like a man should. Support my family like a man. I wonder if I will ever be able to be myself or will I die too pretending to be like a man, even though I am a woman from within.

GCI- You should try to meet other trans people and read or know their stories to understand how they coped up with their struggle.
