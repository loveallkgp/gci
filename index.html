There are days when you are happy. Full of confidence. You see everyone with a smile. And you want to pamper the world. Then there are days when you are at your lowest. Lack of confidence in you makes you miserable. You tend to avoid people by not looking at them due to a fear in your mind that they might end up asking questions which you have been brushing aside for a long time. I, Rishabh Saharan, am passing through these latter days which I stated above . Coping up with a fragile mood is not easy. Everyday living with such thing make one's life a drama. I know about mood swings. They are a regular thing for everybody else but me. They are extreme with me. So I just wanted to enquire about this thing. If this needs some kind of medication or therapy or whatever.

GCI- Sweetie everybody goes through mood swings. I had one last night. What I did was I took some Tiramisu Ice Cream, poured some Maple Cream alcohol on top of it, and ate it while watching Buzzfeed Unsolved's latest episode. Whenever you are feeling sad, try to change your surrounding and your mindset. If you think this is something serious and can be disastrous for you, do seek professional help.
