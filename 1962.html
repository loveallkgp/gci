1962

Hiii .... guys this is Dev Singh here ... I don't think I need to disguise my self because I guess most of my "haters "here know me pretty well .... I don't really know what's wrong in me or what's wrong with my luck ... m I just born to be alone , m I just born to cry ... I know I should control my emotions but how much ... people say me reduce my demands . Reducing my demands will be like .. going out any kind of a guy . Going out with anyone will propose me .. guys I m sick of fighting against my emotions.... you know at times people who are strong even fall short of emotional strength ... dude . I have none who can hug me once and say " dev don't worry I m there behind you " there is no one to hold my hand and swear to never leave me alone . God damn I m a human being too .. every night I cry looking at pictures where you find a gay couples holding hands . Why should it be me .... yaar mene tho kabi kissi ka bura nai chaha tho mere ko hi ithna dard q ...you know ... at times I feel emotionless . I feel numb . I feel like my heart has turned in to a stone .... neither could I cry nor can I laugh ... are bhagawan ji . Agar rulana hi hai tho aap sari rath ik bar me hi rulado ... yaar aise bich bich me akela tho math chodo ....

Guys loneliness kills ... loneliness cut a soul into 2 ....

GCI- Got tonnes to say but I have to keep my patience.
