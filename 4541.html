GCI- Leave him on his own. Don't interfere much in his feelings and emotional space. He is clearly in denial and his regular internal struggle is getting him away from you. If you don't pay much attention to him, he will think about it, he will become restless and maybe then he will have the courage to think about accepting himself. It's called reverse psychology. But do not accept much and don't do anything with him again.
