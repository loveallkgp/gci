Dear YOU, You will never change another person. The only thing YOU can do is walk away. Walk away with the knowledge that YOU were good. Walk away with the knowledge that YOU were true. Walk away with the knowledge that YOU gave it your all. When we keep giving chances over and over thinking someone will be different or change, we get disappointed. Not in them, but in ourselves for falling for something again. When someone betrays us, it's not about us, but about them. It isn't up to you to show them how wrong they were or how good you are or what a mistake they made. They will figure that out down the line. I know its hard, but you just walk away with the wisdom to know that you are everything you should be and everything God has made perfectly. We are defined by how we rise after we fall; not by WHAT made us fall. Love, Me
