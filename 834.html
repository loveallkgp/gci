I feel sad that i am poor. I don't regret being poor because i am but because people kind of discriminate against me in my college. No one likes to befriend a poor, average looking, not so stylish guy and that hurts! Otherwise, i know that its no one's fault if i am poor but the reaction people gives - it just hurts!
