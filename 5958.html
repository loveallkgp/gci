Hello guys. I am a follower of the page , the atmosphere of the page is soaring since a few days. So I just thought to post something that's useful for everybody and entertaining yet. Today I aim to post especially for the guys who want to look perfect. Always remember complexion of your skin doesn't matter the quality matters. Makeup doesn't makes you look less macho or manly. It helps you to look perfectly the way you want . INVEST IN GOOD BRUSHES: You will need few basic brushes. -flat foundation brush(to spread foundation if you wear one) -kabuki brush(to apply powder properly) -concealer brush(most important) -fan head brush (to remove excess powder) -Blush brush (buy it even if you don't wear blusher) -lip brush(must have) -eye liner brush(essential for lip contouring) -eyebrow groomer( must buy) Go for either Vega or MAC Products you must have : You must have a primer, concealer, illuminator, matt bronzer, a lip tint or lip cream, transparent gloss, translucent powder and mascara, eyebrow pencil(must have). Ditch the foundation and compact. Always use a primer( transparent if you are not an expert in colour correction). Its is a cheat trick for a poreless skin. Even if you wear it alone nobody can guess it even if they look with magnifying glass.
