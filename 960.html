Surprisingly i nvr thot it cud b over, Sumhow i managed....to accept d fact !! Im a guy ok... to those who go fr appearence im good lukin guy, yes i approached to gals but nvr ever cud go along wid them in these 4 yrs. But i also knw it wont happen thn he'd bck n i will hug n accept him. He has moved on, but i havent , i waitd for him for abt 5 mnths now. Only i dont laugh much... i dnt knw how to show off bods, they do to impress those cheap gals....who r wid thm today. I dont ineract, i fear if ever i go along wid ny friend (guy) i wud b attached. So i stopped this habit of makin friends now...the guy once hav lots of frnds...now go alone to room. I barely sleep good these days....I wish i cud b wid 1 of tht guy whoz livin my lyf too. The city where im living was once my shimmering mirage, But now its haunted wid memos...Only 2 mnths left im gonna leave this place. Viv- There is so much darkness coming out of your confession. First of all, why do you have to be judgemental about your friends? Thats the reason you don't have any friends. Second, moving on is tough. The more you gloom in the dark, the more sad you are going to be. You are lonely, you had post break up sadness. Am I the only one to see that you are yourself amplifying the melancholia inside you? Try being social the next time you go to college or even out of your home. It can really take your mind out of that darkness. All you need is some light in your life. Get it.
