Viv- First of all you don't have to live for anybody else' expectations. You have to live for yourself. And also do not take the words of the teachers false. They have dealt with hundred of kids and they know how to recognize the talent. If they say that you have a certain talent then there must be something in that. Figure that out and work on your talents.
