My companion of dark hours I want to say this to you;

O' my companion of dark hours, I am lost without you. The night is dark and gloomy And I need you.

O' the courage of my scared soul, I am hopeless without you. The journey is long and destination is uncertain And I need you.

O' the healing touch of my wounded life, I am unwell without you. My life is story of pain and longing And I need you.

I need you like a hope's ray. I need you like the unsaid words that I wanted to say. I need you to walk the dark alley. I need you to smile on a spring day. I need you for every reason And without any reason. I need every day, every moment... Every moment like the air that I breathe.
