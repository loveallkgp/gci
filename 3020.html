it was like a greatest shock, kya yahi pyaar tha, saara saara din ki chatting or proposal, kya yahi tha jise mera dil chahiye tha ...

GCI- That is why don't let the relations happen online. If you do want to get into one then get to meet each other as much as possible and then take things forward only if you feel compatible after some months. This is not a hard and fast rule but if you really want a stability, you have to plan things. Be open from the very start and lay down your expectations. Also, if you are not that smart it doesn't mean that you have to have less self confidence. It comes with your qualities and your achievements, not your looks.
