What is the right age to start looking for a partner? I'm asking this because I've already had 3 painful breakups, I'm 20 now. I find it very difficult to forget people especially after imagining a future with them. I fall for guys easily, wherever I feel I'm comfortable my heart goes out. People take advantage of this and I end up hurt. Is it a sin to care? I care for people, I chat with them, I don't mind listening to their problems and giving them solutions. People misinterpret this for love. What am I to do? Sometimes I feel the arranged marriage set up works perfectly. Look at our parents and grandparents. Somehow our parents know all about us and look for a partner when it's time to be married. How I wish I can shout out- "I'm gay mom- find me a husband." My mom always takes such good decisions, I'm sure she won't let me down. How silly of me!! Sorry if my thoughts are mixed up, I'm not myself at all.

GCI- Its all good buddy. Its all very fine. Everything will fall into place.
