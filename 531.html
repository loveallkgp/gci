there's nothing Gayish in this confession that am going to tell you but it was one of the Happiest moment of my life... So i think I can share with you All...... 5th sem mein Mainey ek subject ke liye bohot Mehenat ki thi... I studied hard for that subject ,... but still mera Exam Accha nahi gaya!!! That day i was so Sad and was depressed too.... Us din mujhey kuch bhi accha Nahi lag raha tha!! I was returning home with my friends when all of a sudden in the midway , i felt as if someone was pulling my hand from the back... When i turned back Mainey dekha ek Chota sa Baccha (probably 5 saal ka) Mera haath kheech raha tha and he was saying "Edikey Aye Edikey Aye in Bengali (Mere Saath Aa, Mere Saath Aa)" He Was so cute with curly hairs and with eyes shining brilliantly.. Us Din wohi ek aesa incident tha jisney mere Chehre par Muskurahat la di.... Itna Pyaara tha wo baccha... mai usey Golu Molu karney hi waala tha but he was with his grandfather .... ye boltey hue wo apney Dadaji or Nanaji k saath chala gaya!!! Mai baad mein ghar akar usikey baarey mein sochney laga aur Man hi Man Has para.. Itna Masoom sa Pyaara Golu Baccha
