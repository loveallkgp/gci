GCI- Many people do build a guard around them in which they feel comfortable. I had that too. The things that were new to you, become boring and a part of the routine. That's when you lose a part of yourself and start drifting away from the population around you.

Maybe try showing some emotions to those you care about. You will see that life is much more than just from 9 to 5.
