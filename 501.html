i knew i am gay when i was in 6th grade now i am in late teens ,till to date i acted macho, rough and tough but i am soft kind hearted and emotional person .the thing is sumtimes i forgot my macho act and papa suspect me being gay.i plan to run away from home and be on my own. Delhi

Viv- What will you do if you run away? Do you have any plans? Any security? Any future? And somehow even if you manage to run away and your parents sent out a search party and you end up in your home once again will you have your freedom? Running away from a problem is never an issue. You should always learn to face it. Become a successful and independent person and no one will ask you how you want to live. Then only your voice will matter. Leave your idea and you will be happy in future, follow it and you will regret it for whole life.
