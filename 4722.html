Hello, I am 29 and stay at Pune, belongs to a small town in U.P. (family stays there and i am here for job)

M out to my immediate family (parents and siblings), even most of my close friend and people at work (few team mates) knows about my orientation.

From last few months i feel v lonely not even want to meet any one or go outside. I have no regret but some how loosing my interest in everything. Although at work front everything ok .

I recently meet a psychiatrist for this and he says everything is normal, i need not to worry. But sometime i think what's going on- I don't want to go party, shopping not even movies. turned down invites from frnd to join for vacation or so. Even not active on dating sites.

GCI : its okay . time like this comes when we start loosing interest in things . i think you need new interests . i mean movies , shopping , party or meeting people or dating sites are so mainstream . i don't know how hectic is your life but maybe (if its not hectic ) .. adopt a pet , get good books , try cooking in weekends and invite close friends to eat with you , or get into more things which brings out your spiritual side ..like where you can actually attach yourself . learn things.

that's my idea .
