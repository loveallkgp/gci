I am a 21 year old student n am thinking of coming out to my elder sister this winter. Am scared n have no idea how c will react. Am not even sure n have been thinking a lot abt this, will it b ok to come out to my sister or is this a terrible idea. It will really help me if anyone have any experience regarding this matter n is willing to share their experiences in the comment section.

GCI-

From our side the suggestions are- 1- See to it that your sis is phobic to lgbt or not! 2- Do think of your self dependent status. 3- Do not think that its gonna be all scary or all hunky-dory. Be prepared for both the situations. 4- Never allow yourself to be weighed down because of your sexuality. Its fine that it makes u what u r but does not define u 100% of what u r capable of. 5- U need to have a blueprint of your future life where u can sustain on your own, with or without a partner. Make yourself ready for that first. 6- U need to consider the fact that who are the other important persons u need to come out to. 7- U also should consider that whether u r strong and mature enough to handle the so called pressures post coming out!

P.S- After all the considerations are clear and checked...go ahead my boy and embrace your truth and let it disintegrate in the air and be all what u r and all that u are yet to become.
