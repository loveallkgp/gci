But that's not the reason am here for. There is this guy at my office. He is quite cool guy. He is out and proud. I always wanted to have such peeps around me. The problem is that I am in the closet and without cing out my chances are zilch. Moreover, he is a Caucasian and am Indian and a Sikh. He likes my company and talks about gay people all the time. Most of my work colleagues (60% Indian) don't talk much to him 'coz he's gay. They even want me to stop having any converstaions with him. But I do like him and want to hang around with him. What should I do guys? PS: Happy Pride Month guys. Hope you all are wearing the rainbow flags. Ciao
