Dude I want to improve my communication skills please help me.

GCI- U will learn it if U r here regularly.

Jokes apart If u really want that then u need to consider these facts- 1- U need to build up your vocab first, including the proper parts of speech to be pitched at proper time and place. 2- U need to talk to strangers(u can start with the page members) virtually or in reality. u can start in anyway. 3- U must not hesitate or show an overtly introvert nature while speaking. Introvert people r NOT under confident, they are just not interested enough to speak and the outcome is them being labelled unsocial. 4- U must write, read and speak i.e Writing makes a precise man, reading makes us a perfect man and conference makes us a ready man. 5- U can also try in front of the mirror first and then gradually have discussions with ur friends over something u r interested in. 6- Striking a convo is a two way process; you must know what to speak and u must also know whether u r able to retain interest in the listener. 7- Always dress up nicely, that helps boost confidence while speaking formally at occasions. 8- Communication skills take time to polish and if u are willing to get cut then only u can turn into a diamond one day.

P.S- Language skills are a pre-requisite for communication skills and it includes both the spoken and the written form. Gluck!
