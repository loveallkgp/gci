I'm gay and I don't give a shit about it.......I don't sleep around with random guys....I don't wage a war against "straights". I don't fit into any of the stereotypes of being gay physically or in my mind. I find myself utterly normal. I don't even like to identify myself solely based on my sexuality because I feel it is just a tiny aspect of the person I am.
What I do like is that I am unique just as each one of us are. I love being aroused by the gender I desire. I yearn for love and to be intimate just like any other. I am gay and I am more than just gay. I am a working, tax paying citizen of my country. I love fine arts. I sing, I paint, I workout, I love charity and volunteering. I love my parents, pets, and I love my country. I hope to find a partner....a partner who could satisfy my intese hunger for companionship and not just an object for momentary gratification. I have met many guys but none have yet fulfilled what I need. I'm on the prowl and will never stop hunting!!
