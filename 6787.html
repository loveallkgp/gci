"I am gay." "Hey, I am gay and I am proud of it." "I am a proud gay." "Being gay is awesome." . . . But, alas, this acceptance by me of what I am is not helping me at all. I may shout aloud the fact of me being gay, and pretend to be cool and happy with it, or claim that I want to live as I am, with my head held high. But, do I have the courage to do the same in reality rather than this virtual world. Do I take a stand on issues which relates to the LGBT's against the odds. The answer which I get from inside is a clear "NO". This is what the reality is. I ask my God, to imbibe courage into me to overcome these. I want to live a 'real' me. Real in reality. I want to be gay and proud. . . . "I am a gay, and proud of it." "Being gay is being you." (The vicious circle, second lap starts.)

M 21, a proud gay (I hope.) Guwahati.

GCI- Not everyone has the courage to be a fighter or to take the stand, and that's okay. If you want to do something then work for it. Don't feel bad about not taking a stand as of now. But at the same time, don't give others a message that they can say whatever homophobic stuff that they can. My stand taking came in me when I started arguing with that homophobe Harit Kumar on facebook.
