Heya Admin! Hey all! bas kuch kehna chahta hu ki badi strong wali feelings aa rhi h ki fir se relationship me jau... Ex k bad, khud dump hone k bad aur ek ko ditch kanne k bad bada risky sa lag rha h fir se iski shuruat karna. Apne sad time me decide kia tha ki kabi relationship me nhi jaunga aur isi wajah se bohot logo dur kar dia. par ab lagta h ki insan hi hu yar time k sath shayad chize achi ho jati h aur shayad im ready to grow in love once again. Koi mila to nahi hai abi tak par bas aise hi thoughts aa rhe h ki purani baaton ko purana hi rehne du. Dil pe patthar rakh ke chahe kinne bi tough upsetting decisions kyo na lu, ek din to zakhm bhar hi jata h... Nahi pata kya karunga bas ainwai random abstract thoughts aa rhe h... Par sath me ek us bande ka sabse zada dukh lagta h jise maine ditch kia... Use ditch karke bura zroor kia tha par I know mai bura insan to nhi hu... Mannnn! Inni saari mixed feelings... Shayad with passage of time iska bi solution nikal hi lunga... Dil fir se dhadakne laga h... Pata nhi baaki... God knows...
