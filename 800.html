How to deal with the inferiority complex of being an effeminate gay like Bobby Darling? In fact, some gay people only hate us for being such! What can then be expected from the straights?

Viv- To be honest I got no problem with feminine guys. Infact one of my straight friend is quite feminine. I am much more macho. It should really not be an inferiority complex. You feel insulted because you yourself are ashamed about it. If you get confident about yourself then no one can throw dirt on you.
