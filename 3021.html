On the topic of relatively unknown health conditions, let me add my contribution. There's a condition called "Testicular Torsion". It is rare but can occur 1 in 10000 males below age 25. I suffered with the condition when I was 12. It happens when the cord connecting to a testicle gets wound up and prevents flow of blood. This causes terrible pain. Many doctors are either not aware or don't consider the problem. Due to this I lost my left testicle (it had shrunk due to low blood supply). If I had got timely treatment I wouldn't have lost it. Guys please read up about this and save yourselves. I sometimes wonder if having only 1 testicle would cause me to be gay.
