I'm 19 now, had a circumcision ( operation to remove fore skin) when I was 7. Doctor advised to wear loose clothes and no tight clothes. So my mom bought some skirts for me, I used to wear to my church along with a nice shirt and make up, which my mom put. Fortunately it was in holidays, if not school would have been hell.

Long after the recovery when I had gone back to wearing shorts, I used to sometimes wear the skirt and dance. I loved how it used to spread out. Thinking of those days make me want to buy a skirt and wear it now. PS: I'm not a cross dresser.
