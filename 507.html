I think most of us are victims of sexual abuse as a child. When I was like 5 or 6, there was this guy aged 20+ something who lived next to our home. He abused me regularly for months. I didn't have the slightest idea back then that I was being molested. Actually I didn't know what he was doing to me! I am 23 now, I don't even remember his face but I still think he was the trigger! This was what turned me gay! I don't regret being gay..but I still feel sad and my eyes gets watery if I think about that guy who exploited my innocence and abused me. Hate him!
