Gay relationships sit on such a loose thread they easily break. Commitment is hard to find, and even if there is, separation is still on cards, maybe in weeks or months or years. My best friend break up after 9 years shattered my hope as well. How will i console him, when i am in state of shock as well and feel dejected about relationships. it happened to him in just starting of his thirties, but if it happens in late thirties, forties or more, what happens next?

Future looks scary to me.
