Realized three things about Gay Life :

  1. Being effeminate / girly makes your life hell.
  2. Loving straight guy makes your life hell.
  3. Coming out to somebody.

Of the LGBT, its only G and T which suffer.

GCI- Lets not undermine the struggle of lesbians and bisexuals. Lesbians because on top of being homosexuals, they are women and we know how suppressed they are sexually because of patriarchy. Bisexuals because no one really understands them. They lie in the middle of the sexuality spectrum but people being people, want to fit everyone in the binary just as they fit everyone in the category of male and female.

Either way if you belong to LGBT and you have struggles with your behavior and your appearance, rather than whining about it, be proud of what you are.
