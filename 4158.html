I have a good gay friend. I feel happy being with him. But may be he is not that happy with me. May be because I am not so manly. But his sorrow hurts me. What should I do?

GCI- Not happy with you? Honey, if he had said those words or you feel through his attitude that your not-so-manly nature irritates him, then try decreasing your communication because you want people near you who make you feel good.

Otherwise everyone of us(not only LGBT but straights also) is fighting a battle deep inside. Ask him if he is going through some trouble. Be a better friend.
