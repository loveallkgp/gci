Being in love with someone is not about clubbing, dating and sleeping with someone. Love is not about having a casual affair and getting physical pleasure out of a relationship. Love is not about short lived sexual fantasies and lust of the youth. True love is to cherish the presence of your lover when you are old and done with your dreams and fantasies. Love is to grow old together. It is all about being connected to someone through your soul till the end of time. It is all about caring for each other when you really need to be looked after. It is all about having a sense of comfort and ease with each other after living together for years and years. Love is to hold each other close to the heart through the worst times of your life. True love is an adventurous journey of two loving hearts connected till eternity.

Viv- (y)
