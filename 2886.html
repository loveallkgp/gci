Feeling weird about gay love:

I thought maybe isse bi mujhse pyar ho gaya. I kissed him on his forehead, hands, cheeks and said,"I love you". he replied with a smiling face, "I know". I kept on repeating myself 3 times but he never said I love you. I wanted to kiss his lips and express my true feelings for him but he said I can but only as a best friend. I refused.

I am kind of a person who always had true and bold emotions for him. my love for him is brave enough to face my parents also. All I always wanted is him to love me back. somewhere deep down he knows he'll never find a guy like me.

I don't know what to do now, since then every second day I cry, I love listening sad songs only. I'm not in depression but I am sticked with him like glue to paper and if I try to severe myself from him, I'll be torn apart :'(

My only wish is to have him forever as my boyfriend, Maa Durga ki kasam kabi ni chodunga usse. I hope someday and very soon he'll understand my love for him is like diarrhoea, it can never stop!

Guys please pray for us.
