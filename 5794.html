Jab me chhota tha to socha karta tha ki kuch log kitne khushnaseeb h jo unhe kitni cheezen muft me mil jati hai. Par ab me samajh paya hu ki kuch bhi cheez is duniya me muft nhi milti, har ek cheez ki kuch keemat hoti h. Kuch logo ke liye, us keemat ki koi ahemiyat nahi hoti par kuch logo k liye vo bhut mayene rakhti hai. Sab ahemiyat, haisiyat or waqt par depend krta hai. Or shayad isiliye me ab samajhta hu ki zindagi me kuch fantasy nhi hoti, hoti hai to bas zimmedariyan. Or zimmedariyan aati hai jab hum dusron k liye jeete hai or tab khatam ho jati hai jab hum khud k liye jeene lagte hai. Hum bhul jaate hai ki ye zindagi humari hai or hume ise apne khud ke nazariye se jeena hota hai. Ab sochta hu ki khud k liye jee lu par yaad aata hai ki sir par haatho se baada sa responsibilities ka bhaar utha rakha hai. Aur agar ab use chhoda to khud hi dhas jaunga.
