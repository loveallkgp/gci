Dilemma....

I meet a guy in fb some three years back....we chat and we become friends....After few months he said he love me....and so i accepted his proposal.....we never meet....we chatted and talked over phone.......but after one year.....our love become cold...so it went like that.... again we meet this year ... we meet each other in person...he said he love me so much.... but he had so many relationship before me...n i threated he might cheat me... i love him so much... whenever i checked his Fb....his wall is full of posts like babe...huni...i love you..with other guys....and when i ask him ...he says that is his best friend....m really in dillema plz help....wheter to trust him or not....Can a guy called his friend huni?

Viv- Oh honey, LDRs are always like this. You always remain in doubt and in this situation I'd say until and unless you both plan to stay in the same city, do not get emotionally involved. You never know what is going on at the other end. But do not be that much doubtful that to ruin the thing between the two of you if he is staying true to you.
