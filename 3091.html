19 #Haridwar

Today on the occasion of Raksha Bandhan me and my family went to our bua's(father's sister) home. I was meeting all of them after a long period say a year or two. We had amazing day with them full of joy, laughs, colors and rakhis.

While we all were making conversations with each other, I had an interesting talk with my fufaji (bua's husband) and felt a kind of zing in my heart. We exchanged our sight a lot many times and whenever we looked at each other passed smiles, I know it's normal but at that very moment I found him very attractive and certainly discovered my liking for him.

Though he's married and have a child but still he's very fit, attractive and handsome.

Many people may find it very weird and completely trash but this is all what my heart felt.

I even know it's just my infatuation but certainly I developed it.

Is this whole thing right ?
