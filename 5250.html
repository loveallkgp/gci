Need your help, advice, suggestions how do i confess to my family, friend & relative tht i am gay ..

GCI- First of all, don't do it under pressure. Just because you feel like you cannot hold it in you, it's not good if your parents are homophobic. Second, know the mindset of your parents. Third, you have to be emotionally, physically and financially safe if you want to come out.

If you know that your parents can accept your sexuality, then go and tell them. If you think that they cannot, wait until you are independent.
