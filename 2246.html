Friends. Today I am very happy to have joined this community. We who are living with alternative sexuality face so much difficulty in our lives. We always want to relieve ourselves from pain and emptiness. But what we get instead ? More frustration !! More pain ? Most of the other GAY sites are filled up with fake people and abuses. It is very frustrating when you want to speak out and the only thing that you get is sex and abuses. I am happy that finally there is a site which takes care of the genuine problems of sexual minorities in a sensible manner. Good Platform for all of us to speak our heart out. Thanks. God Bless you all.
