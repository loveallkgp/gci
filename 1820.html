Its more a question then a confession. if you know the answer please let me know. Thanks

I was broken-hearted. I sat on my bed with tears streaming down my cheeks. My seven year old niece tugged at my pajamas, asking me why I was crying. I quickly wiped my tears away and faked a smile. "Yes sweetheart, tell me what did you want to ask?", I asked her since she had a textbook and pencil in her hand. "Chachu, is there life after death?" she asked, rolling her eyes like only cute little kids can. I smiled a genuine smile this time. Kids are wonderful, I said to myself. They have this uncanny knack of making things better. "No baby. Death is the end of life as we know it. Everything else is superstition," I explained to her. She thought about it for a minute. Then as expected, she asked her follow up question. "Chachu, is there life before death?" she asked. "To this question, my precious, even I do not know the answer," I replied
