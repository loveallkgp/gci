Hi. I am currently in a long distant relationship. He really loves me a lot and I am very lucky, but I cannot carry it on as I feel empty and angry most of the times. I never wanted long distancing but gave it a try becz he ws special. Should I end this? Or hold on to this frustration?

GCI- The question you should as yourself is that why are you feeling frustrated. Is it really because of the long distant relationship or something else? If he is special for you and you feel lucky then what is the problem? Talk about his stuff with him. It should be a mutual decision for you both and not a one sided one.
