Recently i am Young through a rough path. I can'T even figure out what driven in me lately. All i think about is an uncertainity of mg future & struggle that i am gonna have. I was so damn excited about getting my PG degree and do what i love to do. But lately everything seems impossible & blurry from top to bottom. Having a conservative middle class family & struggle that I made in my whole life is pushing me into a darker position. Honestly I don't know what I am writing now & why I am writting..!!! GCI- Honey, you have to take some time and spend it on yourself and find answers. You cannot continue to live with depression and other mental health issues. Also, try to focus on your achievements, rather than the negative stuff in your life that is pushing you down. Life is hard, but it's you who should have the survival's instinct.
