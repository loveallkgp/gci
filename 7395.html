I'm good looking jiske wajah se almost saare non straight gays mujh par hit karne ki koshis karte hain. Because of this, I barely talk to many people and make friends. Logon ki niyat fisal jaati hai mujhe dekh kar. Issi liye maine khud ko sab se door kar liya hai. I dont wanna be a sex object. All i want is ONE guy who would love me for the rest of my life as much as I'd love him. Par yeh khwaish lagta hai shyaad hi muqammal ho payegi kyon ke maine logon se dosti karni chod di hai pichle 4 saalon se (since i turned around 16). Mujhe samajh nahi aata ke kaun banda mujhe lusty nigah se dekhta hai aur kaun love ki. Aalam yeh hai ke ab toh khud se bhi ghin si aane lagi hai. Apni hi shaql se nafrat hone lagi hai. Kabhi khayaal aata hai kay apni shaqal kharab kar loon. Zara toh sukoon milega :( Par phir yeh soch ke ruk jata hoon ke jaane kitne log zara sa behtar dikhne ke liye kya kuch nahi karne ko tayyar ho jaate hain aur jab Allah ne mujhe iss shaql se nawaza hai toh kuch wajah toh hogi hi. Khair, mere masle ka solution dhoondne mein meri madad karein friends. Main apni social life ka kya karoon.

GCIL- 3 things for u.

1- Its ok to be objectified because of ur beauty at times but not ok if ur persona is taken into account as a whole. 2- U must not feel substandard because u r goodlooking, instead u must understand that u have been given an opportunity to feel how to deal thyself being selfish and selfless at the same time. 3- Lastly I would say that there is no need to feel victimised, the good looking people are sought out because of their outer appearance, but always work on ur inside more. That will make a difference.

P.S- U can have a good social life provided u stop bombarding urself with the tag of being handsome all the time. Have a nice life.
