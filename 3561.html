All day I think why I even came to this world? what's the Importance of my existence? Even if I dissapear, there will be no one to care and notice that someone's missing. I have no friends, no one to even talk with me. I tried finding a partner and also had crush on few people but because of lack of desires, it was easy for me to get over them. what i want now , it's not a friend or partner but a place where i can go where nobody finds me and i hibernate forever...
