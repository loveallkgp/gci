Harmless Hugs 13 March 05:41 STREET PLAY: Pehchan and Mard at Eloquence fest'15 Being part of queer community we must have faced discrimination in different phases of life.We have been projected as an instrument of fun or simply faced sheer discrimination. PEHCHAN street play focuses upon different issues faced by LGBT community. Men in our society are considered to be superior to the opposite gender and are conditioned to dominate people around him.MARD street play is one such play that highlights the patriarchal norms engraved in the society.
