My story : Kya yahi pyaar hai..

Dosti ke baare mein ek cheej bahot acchi hoti hai.. Kisi rishtey ko naam dena agar mushkil ho jaaye, to hum uspe dosti ka label aaram se chipka sakte hai.. Duniya ki najron se dekho, to hum dono dost hi the ek doosre ke.. Par meri taraf se hi shayad seemao par lagam na lag saka aur us rishtey ko pyaar ka naam dena bhi muze adhoora sa lagne laga.. Woh mere hi college se tha.. Par pehle baatcheet hui Infosys mein.. Training ke dauran.. Mysore mein.. Fir bhi pehli nazar mein pyaar vyaar kuch hua nahi.. Fir ittefaq se hum dono ek hi project mein aa gaye aur pehchan badhti gayi.. Shuru shuru mein uske sundar chehre ki wajah se usko andekha na kar saka.. Uska gora rang, mulayam baal, chehre ki shobha aur badhati uski hairstyle aur beard.. Aur uska lamba sexy chehra.. 'Tangled' ke Flynn Rider jaisa.. Uske is roop ne meri neendein uda di.. Uske kapdon ka choice bhi accha tha.. Sab range uspe jachte.. Project ke kaam mein to woh accha tha hi, par uski likhawat bhi sundar thi.. Woh sketches bhi anchi banata tha.. Is paripurnata mein kami sirf ek hi baat ki thi.. Uska ghamand.. Muzse bhi ek do baar chhoti chhoti baaton ko lekar jhagda hua uska, par usse dosti mein dooriyan nahi aayi.. Uspe vishwaas kiya aur maine mere saare raaj uske samne khol diye.. Shayad wahi meri galti thi.. Uske baad office ke logon ne muze chidhana shuru kiya.. Dusre ladko ke saath mera naam jodte hue.. Main usse bahot naraj ho gaya.. Maine jo baatein usko itne vishwas se kahi thi, woh aur kisi se na kahe yahi meri iccha thi.. Lekin fir achanak ek din usne muzse kaha.. "Bata.. Tuze kaisa ladka chahiye.. Main dhoondhta hoon ladka tere liye.." Maine kaha.. "Tumhara clone banakar dedo muze.." Ab is baat ko bhala woh kaise seriously leta? Uski sagaai kaafi pehle ho chuki hai yeh baat muze pata thi.. Isliye fir maine poocha.. "Shaadi kab karoge?" Usne kaha.. "2015 mein.." Mere man mein chhupa shaitaan chillaya.. "Matlab abhi aur 3 saal baaki hai.. Usse pehle keh de usse apni dil ki baat.." Sharab pehle kadwi lagti hai.. Fir mastishka ke kone kone mein toofan macha deti hai.. Aur dheere dheere uski aadat si ho jaati hai.. Aisi hi kuch haalat ho rahi thi meri uske saamne.. Uske ghamand se nafrat karte karte main uske isi ada ka deewana ho gaya tha.. Upar se sakht dikhnewale is patthar ke andar jharna tha.. Muzse hi anbhigya mein usmein itna doobta chala gaya ki agar ek din bhi woh office nahi aata to main bechain ho jaata.. Shukrawar ko Pune se ghar aate waqt bus mein ekant mein meri aankhein behne lagti.. Abhi agle 2 din main use dekh nahi sakunga is khayaal se.. Ye itni deewangi kisliye, ye meri khud ki samaz ke bahar tha.. Use ye saari baatein kehkar main mukt ho jaau, ye khayal bahot baar aaya.. Lekin jab woh London gaya, tab uska sketch use gift karne ke alawa main kuch kar na saka.. Aaj bhi use facebook pe "Miss you" se jyaada kuch keh nahi paaya hoon main.. Keh doo use ya chup rahoon, ye sawal to hai.. Par usse badi ulzan ye hai ki.. Kya yahi pyaar hai? Iska jawab muze abhi tak nahi mila hai..
