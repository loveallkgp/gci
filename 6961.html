Hii. In school days, I got to know that I'm gay. I would get uncomfortable with guys (who r presumably straights)-my classmates because of my being gay. Sometimes also because I would be asked questions whose only justified answer is : Bcoz I'm gay. So, slowly slowly I made myself alienated from them. I had no friendships.

Now I'm in 4th year of my college. I live in a boy's hostel. And the same thing continued here.

In first year, I was questioned so heavily about love life, friends, interests, sex life etc. That I got withdrawn again.

Since I'm uncomfortable with guys therefore it's not possible for me to be relaxed when I'm in a group of boys, or if I talk to a bunch of semi-nude boys in my hostel or when 5 guys are there on 2 beds etc.

Lots of people want to talk to me and befriend me. But I get nervous and feel uncomfortable after a while. And since I live in a boy's hostel I slightly fear also that someday something unfortunate could happen. That's why I avoid interactions with people. And it has always been the case.

I never made good friends and avoided to make any. I avoid people before they are so comfortable to ask me about my love/sex life.

Because of all this, my life has become very loenly. There are times when I don't talk to even a single person from day to evening.

Being a closeted guy in a boy's hostel is preventing me from making friends.

Even people ask me, why don't you come and get along with us? Why don't you sit and talk with us?

And some feel I'm arrogant too.

Judging doesn't hurt as much as my loneliness does.

GCI- There is no one to whom you could be yourself? Don't look for romance right now. You need a platonic friendship with someone who knows your reality and is okay with it. You need to be okay with yourself and you need to learn to not give a damn. It took me a long while to reach there.
