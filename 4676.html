Was returning from a lil weekend tour, on Train, on my next row, a family was sitting, as I guessed, they were mon, dan, daughter n son. Middle class. Then came one Trans lady, and was asking for money. Yes, I kept my eyes closed, as if m sleeping, n she passed by my seat, didn't saying anything like 'kya chikne, 10 rs nikal' etc. , aftr reaching my wallet I didn't find much, n M unemployed presently. So I didn't give her any. I looked at her, she was now walking towrads d front rows, with a smile on her face, wearing a sumple black worn off saree, n just a black plastic clip on hair, no claps, no slangs, no forced efiminate behaving, or cursing. She was not wearing nything woolen that would keep her warm, on her right hand was a row of folded10 rs. notes, like bus conductors. She was gping seat by seat, n was saying something quierly, with a gentle smile, leqning her fragile body a bit. whateva she said was not audible to me.

I heard giggles coming from somewhere, n found out, the family next to me, could see frm my seat, the mom n her daughter, a college student may be, were laughing together, pointing to the trans lady.

I dono wat they found funny?! 1. Was it the fact that the lady was not wearing any warm clothes or accessories unlike them?
