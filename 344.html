Life has never been that easy from the time I came out to my parents, cousins, friends. It is not that I am suffering from the pain of social and family stigma for being what I am, it is simply because I came out to most of of them at that very moment when I discovered my sexuality, when I discovered that I had downfall towards same sex.

Like every teenager I started a quest for love among my crushes. It was unfortunate that the ones whom I fell in love with were counted to be my best friends, one from my school days while the second one is from my college itself, and unfortunately both are Straights. I am still in love with my college waala buddy. I care for him like anything and share all my happiness and sorrows with him. And he does too. And he knows that I am Gay, for ofcourse I myself told him this a year back during my II Semester. He is absolutely fine with it. But I am absolutely not fine with the feelings that I imbibe in myself for him. It hurts so badly whenever he lies to me about any of the silliest things which are not a big deal to hide about.

It is inevitable that for the next 2 and a half years of my college life I would not be able to resist loving him but I certainly fear that these damn shit sentimental thoughts of mine are adversely puttimg impact on my stuidies.And despite knowing all this, I just cant help myself coming out of this unbreakable four-walls of fucking love for him.

Because When You Fall In Love For Someone, You Perhaps Do Not Enquire His Sexuality At First. Rather The Shit Happens And You Acknowledge It Later When Its Too Late... when its too late!
