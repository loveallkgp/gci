GCI- You get angry because of irritation. Why you get irritated? Because you get impatient. Anger is the key to all destruction. You cannot remove it but you can control it. There are a few techniques like hyperventilation, deep breathing, counting which can calm you to a certain extent. Also, try not speaking up things to anyone when you get angry. Go to some place where you can boil off in solace. And when you get calm down, you can think with a cool head.
