God..save me I have turned 18 day before yesterday...i want to come out to my family....but hesitation persists... Something is choking me from inside......i am having suicidal thoughts.... I don't wanna hurt my family....i wanna live my life like a free bird..... Please let me know what should i do?

GCI- Obviously you are not ready to come out yet. Don't feel pressurized like that. Sometimes decisions taken in such pressure cannot result in a good outcome. You have to plan your coming out only when you think your parents can understand you or if they don't, your are financially stable. Just don't do it in emotional pressure.
