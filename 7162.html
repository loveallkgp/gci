Lucknow main avi raat k shayad 12 bj rahe hain aur thand bohot hai, par is thand main bhi mujhe apni balcony main khade ho kar samne ki khidki ki or beumeed taakne main koi harz mehsus ni hota.Main yahan hun par mera dil us khidki main hai.Wo beparwah pata nai Laptop main kya kar raha hai.Ek Dafa bhi meri or dekhta bhi nai.Behosh sa kuch likh raha hai ya paper par pen ghis raha hai. Kya pata.Mujhse anjan sa baitha hai. Aur mud kar mjhe dekhne ki shayad chah bhi nahi hai.Sawal hai,wo dekhe to akhir dekhe bhi kyun?Mujhe us se Mohabbat hai use thode hi hai.Wo to bebak sa apni Zindagi main mashgul hai.Wo Zindagi jahan meri koi jagah nai.Main sirf ek "dost" hun. Sirf Hamari Gully ki team main uska "CoPlayer".Aur kya pehchan hai meri? Kuch nai.Kamaskam uske liye to kuch nai hai. Mujhe ek Masla palle nai padta.Agar Main us se apni dil ki baat kahun to wo kahega ewww tum aese nikloge maine socha bhi nai tha.Khak mohabbat hai.Tm Pagal ho.Ilaj karao.Aur Main kya karunga.Kuch ni uski baaten sununga fir chup chap thoda subak kar ghar wapis aa jaunga.Mujhe samajh nai ata agar Mohabbat wo nahi jo main us se karta hun to akhir mohabbat hai kya.Kehte Hain Ishq main sabr hota hai,Main pechle 5 saal se har raat yahan khada ho kar uski nazar ka intezar karta hun wo kya sabr nai.. Ishq main bagawat hoti hai,Main Har majhabi aur Kaydon ki diwar hata kar usse mohabbat ki ab aur kya bagawat karun.. Uski Ek khubsurat mehbuba hai wo bhi thek, wo usse bohot mohobat karti hai wo bhi thek.Umeedan Agle saal Dono shadi kar lenge.Wah.. Aur mera kya? Usne jo chaha wo to use mil gaya. Mera kya? Kyunki Meri Mohabbat main khudgarzi nai to kya iska koi sila bhi nai..Is se behtar hota Allah mujhe ladki hi bna dete kamaskam ye guman to nai reh jata ki kabhi kuch nai bol paya..Is Janam Main Kiye hua har gunah ka ehtaraf karta hun.Ya Allah Mujhe Agle Janam Main Wo banana jise wo mohabbat kar paye..har baat qubool bas us se bichran nai...Aree Wah O Jazbat Meri khamoshi main usne pata nai usne kya suna ek dafa khidki ki or hanth hila diya aur shayad meri ek aur raat roshan kar di..
