I met him through a friend. But, from then, he visits me every now and then. When we are at a party, he drags me apart from others, and likes to dance with me. We go to a bar and we used to talk and play darts. He likes to travel with me. In our group, I am always his point of contact. He shares his personal issues only with me. Despite all these, I am really scared to have an open conversation with him. The sweetest person you can meet. The fear of our friendship broken stops me from doing it.
