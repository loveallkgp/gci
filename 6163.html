GCI- Do what benefits you. He has those particular set of feelings, you have those particular set of feelings which are not getting responded. You are thinking a bit too much on this. I don't blame you as I know how it feels when your feelings are not returned in the same magnitude. But you have to see what's good for you. If you want to make a career, go for it. Don't be like, "Oh I will give things up for him." Trust me, you will regret it later and hate him for it.
