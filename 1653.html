I am working for a LGBT group. The problem i noticed is the non english speaking folks are reluctant to actively take part in community activities. And in India you will always find a class difference. Is there any way we can bridge the gap between us?

Viv- That can be an issue among people but then again I have a few people in contact who are not so good english speakers but they still are in the activism. How to bridge the gap, you ask? Make everyone understand the value of our rights. Make their parents understand the value of human life and the need to stop discrimination. Then only every person, irrespective of their communication skills will take part in the activism.
