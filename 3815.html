Four years ago, I met an 18 year old guy. He was cute, nice and very friendly. He was also a talented pianist and a good singer.

We met on facebook by accident. But we began to chat. Weeks later, we met for the first time.

It was at night, and I met him outside his hostel. I waited for minutes before he arrived. He looked nervous. I still remember looking at him for the first time and thinking, "He looks so young... and he has such a nice smile." He did.

That first night, we sat in the backseat of my car, holding hands and kissing. He was so shy. He could barely look at me. It was adorable.

We met a number of times after that. And eventually, I became the one who took his virginity away.

Things were fine for some time. We were happy. But in the end, we both wanted different things. And I had to let him go...

He eventually found a different guy and moved on...

And as for me, I am still single... by choice. On most days, I am fine. But there are times when I miss him. And during those times, I can't help but wonder if he was the one that got away... and that hurts... <3
