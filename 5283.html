hi main actually aapki rasoi bhojanaliya ki loyal and regular customer hoon. par baat yeh nahi, baat hai ki jo uncle aapki rasoi bhojanaliya ko chalate hai, woh mujhe bohot ache tarike se jante hai aur main wah 12 ssalon se har din khana kha rahi hai. Un uncle ka jo beta hain na, woh gay hai, actually maine use kisi aur ladke ke saath romance karte hue dekh liya tha. fir next day, woh aur uska boyfriend aaya aur unhone mujse kaha ki woh chahte hai ki main unka raaz kisi ko na baatau. Maine kaha koi baat nahi, pyar mein insaan ki rang, jati or uska ling koi mayne nahi rakhta. Ek dusre ko pyar karo aur zindagi ache se jio. undone ne mere paer cho hua aur aashirvad lekar chale gaye. main bas batana chahti hoon ki LGBT log bhi bohot ache hote hain aur insaan agar insaan se pyar kare toh koi buri baat nahi hai, toh main na yaah aagayi sabko batane ki aap chinta mat karo main apke saath hoon. Actually mein 22 ki hoon, M.sc kar rahi hoon toh main in done bachon ko science ki tution bhi deti hoon aur thodi relationship counselling bhi karti hoon. Dono hi bache bohot ache hai. Mujhe bahut pyare lagte hain. challo ab mein chalti hoon, main bhi kabhi kabhi baatein karne beth ti hoon toh choop hi nahi hoti hoon. Yeh meri purani aadat hai, aasha hai maine kisi ko disturb nahi kiya. bye. love you everyone
