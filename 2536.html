In my considered opinion, I feel that cinema theater and film industry should refrain from openly projecting homosexuality. Not because it has a deleterious effect on our culture but because the intentions with which and the manner in which gays are projected is insalubrious to lbtg rights and acceptance. They tend to incite ridicule and scorn in the minds of audience. I hate it when some one makes fun of an effeminate man or a masculine woman. They deserve to be respected not to be derided.

GCI- I guess they should project homosexuality but in a respective way. Cinema is a major source which can help people change the mindset. Otherwise we can see the effect of that cursed film Dostana had till now.
