Ash 19

Manta hu pyar sbko hota h, kisi na kisi ko pyar to ho jata h. Par itna andha bhi nahi hota k apne swarth ke alava khuch na dikhai de.

For example, kisi ko apna khud ka saga bhai pasand aajata h, Kisi ko apna straight friend, kisis ko apna jija, or ki apne shadi shuda friends se pyar kar beth'te hai.

Fir baad me Admin se solution poochte h ke kese seduce kare, kee apni taraf kheeche ya fir keseapne pyar ka izhar kare.

Yar admin se kya poochte ho, ye sawal apne aap se pucho. Kya ye shai h Apne hi sage sambandhiyo se pyar kar bethna for bad me unki shadi sguda zindagi hote hue bhi ye sochna ke kese unko apne pyaar me phasaye.

Saccha premi apne pyar ko paa kar nahi apne pyar ki khushi me khush rehta h. Dosto thoda socho or dimag center me lao.
