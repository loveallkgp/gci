Anyways, I'm got here on a student visa so I am gonna write about that. So it goes like first you have to apply to the university. After you get your letter of acceptance you have to apply for the GIC account which is operated by Nova-Scotia bank. In that account you have to deposit 10,000CAD which will be used when you get here. In the mean time you have to pay the first semester fee and apply for CAQ('CERTIFICAT D' ACCEPTATION DU QUEBEC') if you are entering Quebec province. If its any other province you don't need it. You don't have to show any bank balance or anything else for the visa application. Just your GIC account which is a specific procedure made for Indian students, is sufficient.
