GCI- Hmmmm...very interesting confession indeed...ok lemme put it my way-

1- Love is not limited to loving a single person only. 2- That doesn't mean that we all must be polygamists or polymorphous, it simply means that some people take time to love someone other the same way they used to and some never feel that. Its about the matter of mentality and choice to feel to love again. 3- Love cannot be confined to see only your lover as the ultimate, rather it chooses to be with the one inspite of having options of the same kind. 4- Love also doesn't boast about loving a single person rather it remains a spectator to observe itself even when the person leaves. 5- U must not also think that loving someone again is being perfidious with ur past love, instead it is an accomplishment that u still have a loving heart. 6- U can also take it this way, where u are with someone else too and u love him the same way u used to love ur past bf or partner, that is also loyalty but remember NEVER compare ur present and past love. 7- Love makes new parameters with every new person we love so never use old scale to measure the new intensity of your new found love.
