Hello friends! Mujhe koi suggestion nahi chahiye, bas kuchh confess karna hai.. Meri age 25 yrs hai, aur mai ek ladke se pyar karne laga hu, uski age 17 yrs hai. I know that galti meri hai, mai selfish, meany, or kuchh jyada hi possessive ho gaya tha.. I am feeling very guilty.
