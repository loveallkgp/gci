I ve been disloyal to my bf for an ex and now m just afraid how to talk to my bf n should i tell him that

Viv- That is too complicated. First make up your mind with whom you want to be, your ex or your present bf? Clearly you are not over your ex's attraction and that's why you are sleeping with him and at the same time you are ruining your relationship. Do you feel sorry about it? Do you feel guilty? Can you not repeat the mistake again? If you cannot do any of these things, talk to your ex and have a break up. If you can do it all, still talk to him and ask him for forgiveness.
