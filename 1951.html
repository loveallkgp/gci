It must have been an hour or more that I struggled with some books on GATE that i picked up a story book and sat on the sofa.Three hours flat and i was lost in the book, oblivious to the people in the library around me. The book revolved around a guy who got the first taste of his sexuality at a very young age and then all that follows: non-acceptance, a guy betraying him and getting married to a girl, his seclusion and then falling in love with his student and again getting hurt. It was emotional and painful, most of all, it was a reflection of a life many of us lead.
