Hi folks, This is my first post on this page. I have read a lot of constructive comments and advice. I hope you guys could do the same for me. I was in on and off LDR relationship with a guy for about 4 years. We met just once. And tbh I never felt that I was in love with him. I might have rushed the things. Because I was in terrible position when I met him and online media was my only friend at the moment. He was very and honest but I never had the guts to tell him the truth. I met another guy online and even he is far away. When this new guy asked me to be in relationship with him, I simply said no. But he just wanted me to re-think. Both these guys are wonderful. One is passionate, honest and practical. Other is friendly, adjustable, elite and realistic. I was thinking maybe I am the only one stopping myself to fall in love because of the previous relationship I had. And if that's the case then I don't know how to deal with it. How to break that wall around my heart? And if it's not me then what else is there that keeps me from loving someone. I can't force myself to love someone. It's not under my will. Please help me. I don't know how to cope up with it. I might have sounded bit childish but please overlook that help with honest constructive advice. Thanks!

GCI- Hmmm....Considering ur situation i would suugest few things to be seen...
