1986

Hi, Muje samaj nhi aa rha h ki m kha se shuru kru kyuki bhut depression m hu. Two months later, mera ek friend bna yha pr jo bhut hi sweet h hmne baatein start ki and den no. exchange kiye. Hum dono alag alag state se h. Baad m hum dono ne ek dusre ko real id m add kr lia. Sb acha chal rha tha but vo khi na khi mere se pyar nhi kr paa rha hai reason is oska first love, osne isko itna pain diya tha ki aj ek saal baad b ye osse bahar nhi aa paya h. Aur jb m osse pyar ki baat krta hu to vo frustrate ho jata h, gussa ho jata h. baatein krna bnd kr deta h. Aur rota rhta h. But baad m apne ap hi baatein shuru kr deta hai. But hr baar ye hi bolta h ki mere se kuch expect mat krna m tumhe kbi khush nhi rakh pauga. Aur m apko bta du ki hum dono sex seekers nhi h. Vo muje baar baar ye hi khta h ki tum koi dusra dund lo m kuch nhi kr pauga tumhare sath kyuki m ose nhi bhul skta. But jb se m osse mila hu mera ab aur ladko ko dekhne k mn hi nhi krta, bhut try kiya maine but m ab kisi ko chahta hu to vo bs ye hi h. But muje ye nhi smj aa rha h ki m ose oske past se kaise nikalu jb vo muje oske baare m btata h to muje b rona aata h ki osne isko kitna hurt kiya h.. Guys ye bhut saaf dil k insaan h, m ise khona nhi chahta ise iske past se kaise nikalu ye samaj nhi aa rha h.. Bs m ab ise khona nhi chahta. Help me guys... I love him so much... :(

GCI- Darling, its his will power only that will take him out of his trauma. You can try and try and try but unless he decides to let it go, he can never get over his past. You also have to understand that he is in a bad shape and he needs time for himself. You just be his friend and make him cheer up. Do not force yourself onto him.
