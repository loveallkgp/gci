Hands up for HIV prevention. Theme for World AIDS day 2016. Just a message regarding AIDS present scenario and prevention. Worldwide totally 30 - 40 million people are HIV positive and same no.of people have died so far due to HIV/AIDS. The most common mode of transmission is by sexual route, so let's spread awareness about prevention of HIV, promote use of condoms and other protective measures. We educated ones should create awareness about this in our surroundings and to people who are either illiterate or lacking knowledge about this. And for those who are already infected, start ART immediately to improve the quality of life. So let's join our hands against this disease. Thank you.
